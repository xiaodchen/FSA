What are eligible expenses?

Eligible expenses are medical items or services that count as IRS-qualified purchases you can make with your {{productName}} pre-tax dollars. Only eligible out-of-pocket expenses can be reimbursed to you from your {{productName}}—if they won’t already be reimbursed by the provider—and using your {{productName}} funds for ineligible expenses may result in tax penalties. Below are some of the most common eligible expenses; you can find a complete list here.
